Mass Spectrometry

Chemistry Behind It

Ionization of molecules

Separation by mass-to-charge ratio

Detection of fragment patterns

Applications

  • Proteomics and metabolomics
  • Environmental pollutant analysis
  • Space exploration (analyzing planetary atmospheres)

Benefits

  • Extremely high sensitivity and accuracy for identifying compounds
  • Enables large-scale studies in systems biology and environmental science
  • Supports forensic investigations and pharmaceutical quality control

Safety Considerations

  • High-voltage components require proper insulation and handling
  • Some ionization methods involve hazardous chemicals (e.g., matrix-assisted laser desorption)
  • Samples must be prepared in controlled environments to avoid contamination
