Medical Applications of Spectroscopy

Chemistry Behind It

UV-Vis spectroscopy is used for concentration measurements.

Fluorescence spectroscopy is used for labeling molecules and detecting specific compounds.

Raman spectroscopy assists in tissue and biochemical analysis without extensive sample prep.

Applications

  • Early disease detection (e.g., cancer, infections)
  • Monitoring treatment effectiveness in real-time
  • Research tool for drug development and molecular biology

Benefits

  • Early disease detection
  • Monitoring treatment effectiveness
  • Enables research in pharmacology and biochemistry

Safety Considerations

  • Some fluorescent dyes may be toxic and require careful handling
  • UV light can damage biological samples or eyes without proper shielding
  • Equipment must be sterilized to avoid cross-contamination in clinical settings
